The SaddleSore Rides and Rules

If you are not already a member of the Iron Butt Association, the SS1000 is an entry level ride into IBA long distance riding and will gain you membership of the IBA UK. The ride protocols described in the Rider Guidance document and the Ride Documentation pack must be strictly applied.

The SaddleSore series of rides consists of:

SS1000 1000 miles in 24 hours

SS2000 2000 miles in 48 hours

SS3000 3000 miles in 72 hours

SS4000 4000 miles in 4 days

SS5000 5000 miles in 5 days

SS9000 9000 miles in 9 days

The SaddleSore rides are also available in the metric equivalents, which include K after the number: SS1600K, SS2000K (both in 24 hours); and various other kilometre distance rides are ridden around the world. Basically, rides in the UK are done in MILES while rides in Europe may be done in either MILES or KILOMETRES.

With SS2000 and above there are no restrictions on how the miles are apportioned on the number of days.

SADDLESORE RULES

The basic rules are the same as for other IBA UK rides:

1. Ride the designated distance in the designated time (see below) on a safe route of your choice.

2. IBAUK requires that you document your ride with photographs. The photo must show BOTH the receipt AND your bike's odometer (mileage) reading IN THE SAME PHOTO. Receipts and odo readings must be legible. Record the Start and End details on Ride Documentation 2 and Ride Documentation 3 respectively.

3. Collect fuel and other receipts along your route and photograph each against your odometer. Track them, along with rest stops over 30 minutes, on Ride Documentation 4.

4. Complete all the remaining documentation in the Ride Documentation pack.

5. Copy all completed documentation and receipts and submit the copies, with your name on every sheet.

Additional Rules

SaddleSore 1000

1. Ride 1000 miles in 24 hours or less.

SaddleSore 1600k

1. Ride 1610 kms in 24 hours or less.

SaddleSore 2000k

1. Ride 2000 kms in 24 hours or less.

SaddleSore 2000

1. Ride 2000 miles (3220 kms) in 48 hours or less.

SaddleSore 2000 Gold

1. Ride 2000 miles (3220kms) in 36 hours or less.

2. To qualify for this ride you must have previously successfully completed at least one of the following: SaddleSore 1000, Bun Burner 1500, SaddleSore 2000 or other similar IBA ride. If you are unsure if a ride you have completed is acceptable, please contact verifications@ironbutt.co.uk.

SaddleSore 3000

1. Ride 3000 miles (4830kms) in 72 hours or less.

2. To qualify for this ride you must have previously successfully completed at least one of the following: SaddleSore 1000, Bun Burner 1500, SaddleSore 2000 (or other similar IBA ride) or a Brit Butt Light Rally. If you are unsure if a ride you have completed is acceptable, please contact verifications@ironbutt.co.uk.

SaddleSore 5000

To qualify for this ride you must have previously successfully completed at least one of the following: SaddleSore 1000, Bun Burner 1500, SaddleSore 2000 (or other similar IBA ride) or a Brit Butt Light Rally. If you are unsure if a ride you have completed is acceptable, please contact verifications@ironbutt.co.uk.
